Psalms - Chapter 131

1. <<A Song of Ascents. By David.>> YHWH, my heart isn't haughty, nor my eyes lofty; nor do I concern myself with great matters, or things too wonderful for me.
2. Surely I have stilled and quieted my soul, like a weaned child with his mother, like a weaned child is my soul within me.
3. Israel, hope in YHWH, from this time forth and forevermore.
