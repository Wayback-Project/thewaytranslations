# Genesis + Exodus Alignment Pass (2026-04-08)

Scope requested: ensure Genesis/Exodus align with current Way One terminology direction.

## Checks run
- Divine-title term scan (`Father`, `Father-Mother`, `Cosmic Parent`) in Genesis + Exodus corpora.
- Legacy `repent*` term scan for Exodus chapter context.

## Results
- Genesis contained no Father/Cosmic Parent inconsistencies.
- Exodus had two high-visibility legacy lines using "repent" in divine-action context.

## Changes applied
1. Exodus 32:12
- before: "repent of this evil"
- after: "relent from this evil"

2. Exodus 32:14
- before: "YHWH repented of the evil..."
- after: "YHWH relented from the evil..."

## Why
- Better aligns with source-near and contemporary readability goals.
- Avoids misleading English sense of moral repentance when context is divine relenting.

## Next
- Optional broader OT pass to normalize all divine-action "repent" cases to "relent/turn back" by context.
